Read three texts about a tour. Classify the activities that will be done each day. Click on each box to type your answers and press "DONE" to check the activity. You should take notes.

Days 1-2: Santiago

The flight arrives in the capital, Santiago in the morning. After you check in at your hotel, we will visit the main square and then walk to the top of Santa Lucia Hill for a breathtaking view of Santiago. The next day we will travel to Valparaíso and spend the day ay one of its beautiful sandy beaches.

(Evans & Dooley, 2009, p. 84)

Days 3-5: Atacama Tatio

We will fly to Calama at noon. In the afternoon, we will take you on a tour of the Archaeological Museum. The next morning, we will leave for Toconao. There you will visit the Chaxa Lake and see the pink flamingos of Atacama. We will also visit the Moon Valley with its amazing sand dunes and salt crystals.

(Evans & Dooley, 2009, p. 84)

Days 6-8: Puerto Varas/Santiago

We will fly to Puerto Varas early in the morning. In the afternoon, there will be a tour of the National Park. The next day, we will sail to Chiloe Island. During the crossing, we will see dolphins and sea birds. After our seafood lunch, we will go shopping for souvenirs. The next day we will fly back to Santiago. In the evening, you will head to the airport to fly home.

(Evans & Dooley, 2009, p. 84)
