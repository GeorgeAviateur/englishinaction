Correct the statements based on the reading. Click on the box to type your answer and press "NEXT" to continue. You have two opportunities to answer.

Dear Sarah,

I have some great news. Richard and I are going on holiday for a whole month. Guess where? – Hawaii!

We're leaving on June 1st. We both love swimming, so we're going to stay at a fabulous hotel right next to the beach. I'm going to relax as much as possible because I’m quite tired after working hard the whole year.

We're coming back from our holidays on June 30th, and have lots of plans for the rest of the summer. First, I'm going to work at a bar because I want to earn some extra money and buy a new car. I'm also going to join a gym to lose some weight. Finally, I want to find a better job in the fall, so I'm going to start a computer course.

Oh, I nearly forgot! On August 16th we are having a garden party to celebrate Richard's birthday. Please try to come.

Well, that's all for now. Hope to see you soon.

Love,

Jenny
