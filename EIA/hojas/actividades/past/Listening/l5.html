To help develop listening skills for specific information.
Listen to the conversation. Then answer the questions, Choose a, b or c and press “NEXT” to continue. You can listen to the audio three times, but you have just one opportunity to answer. It is necesary that you take notes.

A. She was at the market.

B. She was at the cinema.

C. She was at home.

A. A comedy film.

B. A Science fiction film.

C. A horror film.

A. Great.

B. Boring.

C. Horrible.
