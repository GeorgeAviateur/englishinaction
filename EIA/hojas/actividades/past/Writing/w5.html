To strengthen the writing ability of the student by writing short texts.
Form the correct question for the answers given. Click on the box to type your answer and press "NEXT" to continue. You have two opportunities to do the activity.

  • What did you do yesterday?

    I went to the cinema.

  • Where were you last Saturday?

    I was in Bogota with my dad.

  • Was your mother at home last night?

    No, she wasn't. My mother was at the supermarket.

  • Where did you and your family go on last vacation?

    We went to Medellin.
