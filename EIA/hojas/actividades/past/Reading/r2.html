To strengthen reading ability through different kinds of exercises.
Read three texts about holidays. Classify people's activities during vacation. Click on each box to type your answers, and press "Check the answers" to verify the correct answers. It is necessary to take notes.

Luis and Sarah

We are Luis and Sarah, we are siblings. Last vacation we went to Paris. We visited different touristic places, such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre museum. Those places are amazing.
We didn’t go shopping because we didn’t have much money.
Paris’s night life is also great! We went to a bar and met some people. We talked about our culture and learnt about their culture as well.
The food in Paris is delicious. We ate some typical dishes: ratatouille and crepes. We didn’t try foie gras though.

We had a great time in Paris. It was an amazing experience.

Emma

I'm Emma and I'm a student. I spent my last vacation at my grandparents' house in a small town called “La Vega”. There I relaxed a lot. I talked to my grandparents, they told me hundreds of stories, it was really nice.
At nights I visited some friends that live there; we watched some movies at their house. We watched horror and action films. We also ate popcorn and hot dogs, and drank coke.
I didn't exercise because there isn't a gym.

I was really happy on vacation.

James

Hello my name is James and I’m a musician. I spent my last vacation working. I traveled to different towns with my band playing at bars. We went to the festivals in Huila and Tolima. There we met a lot of people and made them happy with our music.
After each performance we had some free time and we had fun! We danced and drank some beers. We didn’t have much time to visit touristic places. We just worked and had fun.
I had a great time!
