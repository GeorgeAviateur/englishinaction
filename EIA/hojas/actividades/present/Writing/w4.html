To strengthen the writing ability of the student by writing short texts.
Read the text and correct the statements based on the reading. Click on the box to type your answer. Then, click on "NEXT" to continue. You have two opportunities to answer each exercise.

Hi! My name is Rita. I'm eighteen years old and I live in Israel. I love dancing. It's my favorite hobby.
I don't like ballet, but I like salsa. I go to salsa lessons after school. The teacher is really good!
At the end of the year, we have a salsa show. We have a lot of fun, and everyone says we were very good. We make a lot of money from the tickets. The money is to help sick children.
Dancing takes lots of time. Sometimes I don't have time for my homework. And I want to watch television, but I don't have time. That's is my biggest problem.

  • Answer: Rita is eighteen years old.

    Rita is nineteen years old.

  • Answer: Rita lives in Israel.

    Rita doesn't live in Israel.

  • Answer: She likes to dance salsa.

    She doesn't like to dance salsa.

  • Answer: She doesn't like ballet.

    She likes ballet.

  • Answer: Rita doesn't have time to do her homework.

    Rita has lots of time to do her homework

  • Answer: The money they collect from the salsa show is to help sick children.

    The money they collect from the salsa show is to help rich people.
