My last vacation

Hi everyone! I want to tell you about my last vacation. I had a month of vacation. My family and I traveled to Cartagena. In Cartagena we visited many touristic places. We went to "San Felipe Castle", "The walled City or Ciudad Amurallada". There we learned some history about the city.
My brother doesn't like fish, so we went to fast food restaurants and ate hamburgers, hot dogs and sandwiches, while my parents ate fish.
At night we went to different bars and had some fun! We danced and met many people.
I really enjoyed my vacation, it was great!
